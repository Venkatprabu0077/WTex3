Welcome to Women's Premier League

Both Mumbai and Delhi are the in-form teams this season with their captains leading from the front. Meg Lanning (142) is currently the top run-scorer in the tournament while Harmanpreet Kaur (65) also has a fifty to her name. In-form Mumbai opener Hayley Matthews also has 124 runs to her name in 2 matches and is currently the second highest run-scorer of the season.

Latest News

The Women’s Indian Premier League (IPL) has become one of the most popular T20 leagues in the world, with talented players from all over the globe showcasing their skills. The tournament, which started in 2019, has seen two successful seasons so far, and fans are eagerly waiting for the next edition in 2023.As of now, the official Women’s IPL schedule 2023 has not been announced. However, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has confirmed that the tournament will take place sometime in the first half of the year.
